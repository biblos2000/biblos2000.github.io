Biblos 2000 Professional
is a software developed for the comprehensive automated management of libraries. Ideal for both personal (home) and specialized and institutional libraries; to keep a reliable record of the cultural heritage that is available, as well as the technical and circulation processes that can be carried out around the documents.
Now with version 5.1; It includes both the possibility of networking using MySQL databases, as well as the advantage of easily configuring an online OPAC to provide greater quality and consistency in information retrieval services.


[TECHNICAL INFORMATION]



What can we do with Biblos 2000 Pro?

Cataloging

It allows the unequivocal and unambiguous description of the documents, providing the necessary elements for their identification and adjusting to the regulations that regulate the process (RCAA 2, ISBD).

Classification
To describe the main topic of a document based on the standardized classification language that the documentalist selects, it has the tool by which the main topic is determined and the corresponding index (notation) is assigned for that topic.

In the Configuration module you will find that you can adjust the system for classifying materials using two options:
- CDD (Dewey Decimal Classification, short version)
- CDU (Universal Decimal Classification, abbreviated version 2004)
The second option corresponds to the abbreviated Universal Decimal Classification 2000-2004 edition, the use of which has been authorized by AENOR.
To perform a methodical INDEXING, the program puts at your disposal the alphabetical edition of the UNESCO THESAUR.
Queries (OPAC)

It enables information retrieval through a simple, intuitive and effective search. It can be done locally; or set up a website to check online. (See requirements in the PDF Manual)

Circulation

Register and organize the loans made and the current users of the information unit.

Setting

It offers customization tools for Byblos 2000 Pro and its main functions. Includes backups, selection of the classification system, export of records and maintenance of databases, inventory, statistics, etc.


Who is it designed for?
It has been created to be implemented as a basic tool for the automation of library technical processes by:

* LIBRARIANS or related technicians,
* ADVANCED STUDENTS of Library Science,
book collectors,
* PEOPLE in general interested in managing their collections automatically and efficiently.


Who is responsible for its creation and development?

It was designed in an event-oriented programming environment, using the NeoBook program (currently VisualNEO). The official developer is Ricardo Martín Contard, an Argentine documentary librarian technician; currently, professor in Classification and Indexing, in the Faculty of Management Sciences of the Autonomous University of Entre Ríos (UADER).

- PERSONAL version: for home and personal use,
- NET version: ideal for work in networks

Both versions have a cost of A SINGLE PAYMENT of $10.000 pesos (ARS) or U$S 40 dollars (USD); which includes constant technical support from the developer.

Even so, you can download a TRIAL VERSION for free (with which you can explore the program by registering up to 20 documents or items) and then request the FULL VERSION, to which we will give you access after paying the corresponding cost.
